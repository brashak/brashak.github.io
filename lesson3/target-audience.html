Who will be using this site?

Anyone needing to know the forecast for planning activities, like skiing or hiking, or just needing to know what the day holds for them weather wise.

Who?

Anyone wanting to check the weather for sun or snow conditions.

Age?

10+ or anyone who can readily access and use a device.

Income?

All range of income

Personas

Hunter Smith. He is 19 years-old and an avid snowboarder that hits the slopes at least 5 times a week during winter and works part time down in a gift shop in Park City. Fake snow made by the ski resorts is not his favorite, he wants fresh powder and the only way to know that is if the weather forecast shows heavy snow. He is the Age where it is commonplace to have a phone stuck to your hand, and he uses it often to record himself going down the double diamonds.
