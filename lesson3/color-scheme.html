Color Scheme for Weather Site

The color scheme I will be using for Sun and Snowy, is pallete that can represent the cool and warm parts of weather. They are bright, and pale enough to not be obnoxious.

Website Background - #FFECD1

Header and Footer - #FF7D00

Navigation Bar - #15616D

Secondary Text - White

Primary Text - Black

Used for navigation transitions - #78920F
