Weather Site Name

Name of website is "Sun and Snowy Weather". The domain name will be "sunandsnowy.com", which is $12/year on Google Domain.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to show the weather in major areas of a state that experiences all four seasons. The areas of focus will be Salt Lake City, Park City and St. George. When someone checks out the site, they will be able to see current forecasts in all cities. There will also be forecasts for chance of snow, rain, humidity levels. In a state where snow can be the factor on if you will be driving or not, the site will also be linked with road conditions.
