Who will be using the site?

Anyone needing to know the weather/forecast while in the state of Utah, whether it is for outdoor activities or just passing through.

Age? Income?

10 +, or anyone that can readily access and use a device to look up our site.
All ranges of income.

Scenarios

I want to go on a hike, can I see what the weather is going to be like? Yes, the weather site will show you what the forecasts will look like.


Will I be able to view the weather site on different types of viewports? Yes, the site will be available on phone, tablet and desktop.


Will I be able to see more than just today's forecast? Yes, Pinpoint Weather will show the the whole week's forecast so you can stay updated and informed.


Will the weather site be easy to read and easy to navigate? Yes, user friendly is the most important aspect when accessing out site.


Will I be able to see what traffic is like as well? Yes, the local traffic updates will be linked to our website. Weather can impact traffic and we want our users to have as much information as possbile so they can travel safely.
