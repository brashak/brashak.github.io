Weather Site Name

Name of website is "Pinpoint Weather". The domain name will be "pinpointweather.com", which is $12/year on Google Domains.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to show up to date and accurate weather for the larger counties/areas of the state-- Salt Lake City, Park City, Logan and St, George.When someone logs onto the site they will be able to see current forecasts in all of the mentioned cities. Also because we get all four seasons, there will be chance of snow, rain and humidity levels that will change throughout the year. The site will also be linked to the local road condition and traffic website that can be very helpful during winter time.
