Snow in forecast for much of East Idaho

The National Weather Service has issued a winter weather advisory and special weather alert for much of East Idaho... The forecast includes snow and pockets of blowing snow, snow accumulations of 2 to 6 inches at lower elevations, and 5 to 12 inches in the mountains. Along with winds gusting 30 to 40 mph at times. Plan on slippery road conditions. The hazardous conditions could impact the morning or evening commute. Slow down and use caution while traveling. The latest road conditions can be obtained by calling 5 1 1.Idaho State Journal
