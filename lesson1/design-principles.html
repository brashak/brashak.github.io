Design Principles

Alignment

Sprinkles Cupcakes

Besides being addictive cookies, the Suss Cookies website shows a great use of alignment by centering everything from the content to the text.

Contrast

Doc Martens

The Doc Martens website does a great job at contrasting their colors to make a site that pops and is memorable. They have kept to their brand of yellow and black and it has worked for them throughout the years.

White Space/Clean Design

Free People

Free People does a good job at using the white space as not only design but also the way they break up the different areas of their website and links. They also have a clean and simple design by sticking to light colors and simple font styles.
